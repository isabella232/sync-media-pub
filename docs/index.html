This document is an overview of the work done in the Synchronized Media for Publications Community Group.

This draft is still under consideration within the Synchronized Media for Publications Community Group and is subject to change. The most prominent issues will be referenced in the document with links provided.

Introduction

The Community Group was formed to recommend the best way to synchronize media with Web Publications and other document formats being developed by the Publishing Working Group, in order to make the publications accessible to people with different types of reading requirements.

The recommendation below has been developed after collecting requirements and analyzing various potential solutions.
