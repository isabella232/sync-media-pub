This document is an overview of the work done in the Synchronized Media for Publications Community Group.

This draft is still under consideration within the Synchronized Media for Publications Community Group and is subject to change. The most prominent issues will be referenced in the document with links provided.

Introduction

The Synchronized Media for Publications Community Group was formed to recommend the best way to synchronize media with document formats being developed by the Publishing Working Group, in order to make publications accessible to people with different types of reading requirements.

The documents below have been developed after collecting requirements and analyzing various potential solutions.
